# Domain Glossary

Last reviewed: 2026-05-21

- demande: initial request submitted by postulant or registered from physical channel
- courrier: official letter record tied to demande and DG circuit
- orientation DG: MVP decision outcome (pending/oriented_to_dn/rejected); redirected remains legacy/deferred compatibility only
- dossier DN: operational case opened after favorable DG orientation
- postulant: external applicant representative
- organisme: applicant organization name
- workflow OMA: 5-phase certification workflow (preliminary -> delivery)
- phase: one OMA workflow step with status and due dates
- document: file/evidence record tied to demande/dossier/phase
- reunion: meeting event with outcome and optional report document
- convocation: meeting invitation trace (date/channel)
- paiement: invoice/payment proof evidence kinds
- certificat: certificate lifecycle artifact tied to dossier
- retrait: certificate collection/remise step
- notification: user-facing event/evidence indicating status update
